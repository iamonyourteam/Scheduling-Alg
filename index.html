Overview

Problem

Customers will place service calls throughout the day, and these calls will arrive sequentially over a 5-day work period. Each call represents a task that requires a skilled technician to complete. The goal is to automatically schedule these service calls in the most efficient way possible.
To optimize scheduling, several key factors must be considered:
Technician Availability: Technicians may have different working hours and time-off schedules.
Task Proficiency: Each technician has varying levels of skill or certification for specific types of service calls.
Geographic Proximity: The distance between the technician and the customer location should be minimized to reduce travel time and improve efficiency.
